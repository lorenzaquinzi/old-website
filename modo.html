Studio Mod.o

Bringing together three different visions in a single brand identity.

branding.
graphic design.

Context.

Studio Mod.o is an architecture studio based in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The studio is formed by a team of three architects with different experiences and points of view. Together they are able to create sophisticated, functional, and contemporary projects.
@studiomod.o

Process.

We created their brand name merging two concepts: 'modificar' (to modify, in Portuguese) and 'modo' (mode, in Portuguese).
Modify because is one of the main skills of an architect, since they can change an environment to better suit its use and Mode to represent each point of view inside their team.
We have also created a logo and visual identity that communicate the sophistication and contemporaneity of their projects.

Role.

This project was a collaboration with @biamilhomem.

Together we work as the design collective Northwest.

We shared the same responsibilities in every part of this project: naming, logo design, visual identity, and presentations to the client.

Results.
