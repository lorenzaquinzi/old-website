TRIDI Arquitetura

Brand identity for a young architecture studio.

branding.
graphic design.

Context.

TRIDI is an architecture studio founded by two young professionals. Their work is very clean and contemporary and they needed a visual identity that would communicate their essence.
@tridiarquitetura

Process.

We translated their essence with a very clean and geometric logotype, inspired by floor plans used in architecture projects.

Role.

This project was a collaboration with @biamilhomem.

Together we work as the design collective Northwest.

We shared the same responsibilities in every part of this project: logo design, visual identity, collateral materials, and presentations to the client.
